Part 1. How to convert hevc videos for Samsung Galaxy Star 2 on Windows?
To convert hevc videos for Samsung Galaxy Star 2 playback on Windows, do as below: Step 1: Import video files Click “Add File” to load video files that you want to convert into this program. Step 2: Select output format for Samsung Galaxy Star 2 Click "Profile" to select "Samsung" > "Samsung Galaxy S5(*.mp4)" as output format. This format profile will ensure a good performance while playing with Galaxy Star 2. Tips: If necessary, you can click "Settings" button to modify video and audio parameters like encoder, resolution, frame rate, bitrate, aspect ratio, sample rate, and audio channels. You can also “Enable 3D Settings” to add 3D effect to your source media. Step 3: Click “Convert” to start hevc video to Samsung Galaxy Star 2 conversion on Windows. As soon as the conversion is complete, click “Open Folder” to get the generated MP4 files that are suitable for playing on your Samsung Galaxy Star 2. No audio lost issues will occur any more.
