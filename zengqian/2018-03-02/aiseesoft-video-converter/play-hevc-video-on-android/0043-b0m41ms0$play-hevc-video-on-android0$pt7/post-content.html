Part 1. How to convert hevc videos for Samsung Galaxy Note Edge on Windows?
To convert hevc videos for Samsung Galaxy Note Edge playback on Windows, do as below: Step 1: Import video files Click “Add File” to load video files that you want to convert into this program. Step 2: Select output format for Samsung Galaxy Note Edge Click "Profile" to select "Samsung" > "Samsung Galaxy S5(*.mp4)" as output format. This format profile will ensure a good performance while playing with Galaxy Note Edge. Tips: If necessary, you can click "Settings" button to modify video and audio parameters like encoder, resolution, frame rate, bitrate, aspect ratio, sample rate, and audio channels. You can also “Enable 3D Settings” to add 3D effect to your source media. Step 3: Click “Convert” to start hevc video to Samsung Galaxy Note Edge conversion on Windows. As soon as the conversion is complete, click “Open Folder” to get the generated MP4 files that are suitable for playing on your Samsung Galaxy Note Edge. No audio lost issues will occur any more.
